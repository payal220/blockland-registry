Why Blockchain for Land Registry?
Decentralised StorageNo single server can be hacked or altered. Records exist across thousands of nodes simultaneously.
Immutable HistoryEvery ownership transfer is permanently recorded. Fraudulent claims are instantly detectable by anyone.
E-CertificatesPaper certificates can be lost or forged. Digital certificates with cryptographic signatures cannot be duplicated.
AI Fraud DetectionAI analyses ownership patterns in real-time and flags suspicious transfers before they are confirmed on-chain.

Enter land details below. The AI analyses ownership history, transfer frequency, and anomaly patterns to flag potential fraud before registration is confirmed on-chain.

How AI Fraud Detection Works
1. History Analysis Checks how many times land has changed hands recently. Unusually rapid transfers are a red flag.
2. Value Anomaly Compares claimed value against market rates for that district. Extreme under/overvaluation triggers alerts.
3. Identity Cross-check Validates if the claimant's ID matches existing records to catch duplicate or stolen identity fraud.
